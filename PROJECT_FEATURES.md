# ForexStockX - Project Features & Summary

**ForexStockX** is a high-performance, professional-grade trading simulation platform designed specifically for the Indian market. It combines international currency exchange with stock market prediction in a single, executive-style dashboard.

#### 1. **Live Dashboard & Portfolio Analytics**
*   **Real-Time Net Worth**: Instant tracking of total assets (INR balance + live stock investments).
*   **Dynamic P&L**: Live calculation of your daily profit and loss based on market movement.
*   **Portfolio Mix**: Visual representation of asset allocation between liquid cash and market investments.
*   **Risk Analysis**: AI-driven risk scoring (Low to Moderate) based on your investment patterns.

#### 2. **Professional Forex Terminal**
*   **Live Marketplace**: Real-time exchange rates for global currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, etc.) against INR.
*   **Instant Swaps**: Zero-delay conversion from INR to foreign currencies with bank-grade validation.
*   **Confirmation Engine**: Immediate visual feedback and transaction settlement reports.
*   **Global Balances**: Secure tracking of your various world currency holdings.

#### 3. **AI Stock Prediction Engine**
*   **High-Growth Recommendations**: Curated list of Indian market leaders (Reliance, TCS, HDFC, etc.).
*   **Predictive Insights**: AI sentiment analysis (Bullish/Bearish) and predicted market direction (Up/Down).
*   **Smart Indicators**: Detailed metrics including Confidence Scores, Target Prices, and Time Horizons.
*   **Live Price Movement**: Simulated dynamic pricing that fluctuates every 5 seconds for a realistic terminal experience.

#### 4. **Executive Trading Journal**
*   **Multi-Asset History**: Dedicated ledgers for every single currency exchange and stock investment.
*   **Detailed Records**: Precise logging of entry prices, amounts, timestamps, and target predictions.
*   **Search & Filter**: Easily track down past trades and performance metrics.
